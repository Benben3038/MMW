PrismPress

Learn how geometry is applied in photography to tell a journalistic story

About

PrismPress was created as a simple website that teaches how to utilize geometric sequences in taking photos that tell a journalistic story. Web Name aims to help photographers, Broadcasting and Journalism majors, and campus journalists. With the authors being college students Web Name was also created in partial fulfillment of the subject Mathematics in the Modern World
